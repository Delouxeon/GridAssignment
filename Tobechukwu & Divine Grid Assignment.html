Quotes



"Success usually comes to those who are
too busy to be looking for it"
...Henry David Thoreau

"Success is not the key to happiness.
Happiness is the key to success.
If you love what you are doing,
you will be successful."
...Albert Schweitzer

"I never did anything worth doing by accident,
nor did any of my inventions come indirectly through accident,
except the phonograph. No, when I have fully decided that a result is worth getting,
I go about it, and make trial after trial, until it comes."
...Thomas Edison

"I cannot give you the formula for success,
but I can give you the formula for failure,
It is: Try to please everybody."
...Herbert Bayard Swope

"Success seems to be connected with action.
Successful people keep moving.
They make mistakes, but they don't quit."
...Conrad Hilton
